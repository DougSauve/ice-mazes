Things that can show up on the board:

I white block - ice
W black block - wall
G textured block - gravel

H spiral - hole
X x - landing spot

B squared circle - boulder
P mist - pit

L ladder

(S) stalagmites - colored **
* blasting spell - colored (removes stalagmite)
R rising spell - colored (grows stalagmite)
T time spell - colored, shaped time shown (number of moves before lock closes)

<^>v double arrow - jump
 arrow with number - jump that far (numbers are below in a second table)

{ - entry (after tile id)
} - exit
- outer wall


** between two tiles - (another layer, absolute positioned?)
